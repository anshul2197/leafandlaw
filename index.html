About Us

Our consultancy specializes in obtaining Tree NOCs (No Objection Certificates) from municipal and planning authorities, an essential legal requirement for construction, redevelopment, or infrastructure projects involving tree felling, transplantation, or new plantations.

What is Tree NOC?

Tree NOC is an official approval issued by the local governing body (such as BMC in Mumbai) granting permission to cut, transplant, or retain trees on a project site. It ensures compliance with urban greenery regulations under local Tree Preservation Acts.
